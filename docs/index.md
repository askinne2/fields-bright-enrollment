## How This System Works

Your enrollment system is designed to be simple yet powerful:

1. **You create workshops** with dates, prices, and capacity limits
2. **Visitors browse and enroll** on your website
3. **Payments are processed** securely through Stripe
4. **Confirmations are sent** automatically to both you and the customer
5. **You manage everything** from your WordPress dashboard
